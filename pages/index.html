About us

Freight Transport Bureau,

Department of Land Transport.

The Department of Land Transport is committed to enhancing and regulating the freight transport system to meet internationally recognized standards. It actively promotes the continuous development of transport operators, aiming to ensure services that are safe, high-quality, and reliable. Furthermore, the Department serves as a key driver in advancing Thailand's logistics industry toward stable and sustainable growth, propelling the nation forward with an efficient transport system across all routes.
