Road Freight Transportation Information

About us

Freight Transport Bureau,

Department of Land Transport.

The Department of Land Transport is committed to enhancing and regulating the freight transport system to meet internationally recognized standards. It actively promotes the continuous development of transport operators, aiming to ensure services that are safe, high-quality, and reliable. Furthermore, the Department serves as a key driver in advancing Thailand's logistics industry toward stable and sustainable growth, propelling the nation forward with an efficient transport system across all routes.

READ MORE

Freight Transport Operations Division

Oversees, regulates, and ensures that freight transport operations comply with applicable laws and established standards. The division is responsible for formulating relevant regulations, supervising cross-border freight transport, and developing the electronic licensing system (DLT E-Transport License) to enhance convenience for service users.

READ MORE

Freight Transport Development and Promotion Group

studying, analyzing, planning, and developing the road freight transport system

enhancing the capabilities of transport operators and personnel, developing transport standards ( Q Mark and Q Cold Chain)

“These efforts aim to improve the quality of freight transport and strengthen the national logistics system.”

Freight Terminal Division

Responsible for planning, developing, and overseeing freight terminals, including related infrastructure and facilities. The division also promotes investment and collaboration with the private sector to modernize and enhance the efficiency of freight terminals.

READ MORE

Truck Operation

Truck operations involve the regulation, licensing, and oversight of freight transport by road to ensure safe, legal, and efficient movement of goods. The Department of Land Transport manages and monitors truck operators under both domestic and international frameworks.

Q Mark Standards

The Q Mark is a quality certification standard developed by the Department of Land Transport, Thailand, aimed at raising the standards of road freight transport services. It serves as a mark of excellence, certifying that a transport operator meets the required criteria in areas such as safety, reliability, service quality, environmental responsibility, and professional management.

Q Cold Chain Standards

The Q Cold Chain is a specialized quality certification standard developed by the Department of Land Transport, Thailand, to ensure high standards in the transportation of temperature-sensitive goods, such asfood, pharmaceuticals, and other perishable products.
